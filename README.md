I am Head of AI and Engineering and the founding engineer at **Workhelix**, where I build enterprise agentic and LLM platforms from zero. My work spans LLM serving and inference on self-hosted GPUs, agentic workflows, MCP integrations, RAG, evaluations, and the developer tooling around them. That platform now runs inside Fortune 50 companies like Nasdaq and John Deere.

I am also the **O'Reilly author on AI agents and agent memory**, an AEA-published researcher, and a former university machine learning instructor. I like turning hard systems into clear ideas.
